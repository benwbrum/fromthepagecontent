As crowdsourced transcription projects gain traction at libraries, archives, research projects, local history societies, and elsewhere, a body of scholarly literature has developed around this increasingly prevalent 21st century activity. Here we provide a basic bibliography for those interested in learning more about the theory behind and emerging academic study of this access strategy.

If you have feedback and/or know of other articles or studies, please share in the comments! This post, while making no claims to be an exhaustive listing of scholarly activity on this topic, can nevertheless serve as an additional node of collaboration for the growing, international community of  transcribers.

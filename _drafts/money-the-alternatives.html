Run as a business
justify time spent based on future revenue
acquire funding
benefits:
funding would allow full-time work
cons:
funding is unlikely due to market
market unlikely to pay off
loss of control, including sale of software rights
revenue-driven development (include william's suggestion)
difficulty of giving things away

Get a grant
benefits:
funding would allow full-time work
independence unlikely to be compromised
cons:
grant application may require as much work as could be accomplished otherwise
no credentials -- grant approval unlikely
not that much money -- equivalent to 3-6 months salary

Institutional affiliation
Business affiliation

Both of these scenarios involve persuading someone else to hire me to build the Renan System. They couple a loss of control with the unliklihood of success.

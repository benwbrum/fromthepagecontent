About ten years ago, I wrote for a group blog called Horizon.  Contributors came and went, but a core of us--mostly regulars from the alt.books.george-orwell usenet newsgroup--kept up a good conversation for three years.  Sometime in the last fifteen months, the site went offline.  Although we closed the doors five years ago, I didn't get a back-up and I wouldn't be surprised if none of my fellow bloggers did either.  (Thank you, Internet Archive!)

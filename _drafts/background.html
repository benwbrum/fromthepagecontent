The project arose as a side-effect of my attempt to transcribe my great-great grandmother's 1932 diary. My father's previous effort to transcribe, print, and distribute her 1918 diary was an overwhelming success: the copies he gave to our relatives were passed around to friends and neighbors, taking on a sort of photocopied second life. This success presented difficulties, however: demand for the book outstripped the numbers he printed and every year or so either he or I get inquiries from people who want to purchase more copies.

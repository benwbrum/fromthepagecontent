You can download a letter documenting your volunteer hours on FromThePage by clicking on "Dashboard" and following the "Your Hours" link on the bottom right side. Remember that it can take 24 hours before you see today's activity!

If you need a letter directly from the institution, many of the organizations that run projects on FromThePage are willing to document the time spent transcribing and reviewing. Since each organization documents your service time separately, you’ll want to focus on a single organizations' projects – or ask multiple organizations for documentation!

Thank you for helping out!

Here’s who is currently documenting service hours:

Indiana State Archives = Contact JKoepke@iara.in.gov

Library of Virginia = Contact makinghistory@virginiamemory.com

Maryland State Archives = Contact liz.coelho@maryland.gov

Missouri State Archives = Contact archvol@sos.mo.gov

Sandy Spring Museum = Contact lfraser@sandyspringmuseum.org

Follow the links to find their active projects.

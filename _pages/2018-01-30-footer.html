You can customize the footer in your FromThePage collections via the settings tab. 

A simple footer can just be text containing your institution name and how to contact you.

You can also use HTML in the footer field. (If you're not comfortable in HTML you can convert a Google Doc to HTML using this website. After pasting your content in, click on the <> icon and copy the results into FromThePage.)

FromThePage has been funded by subscribers, with major features sponsored by the following organizations and funding agencies.

University of Texas Libraries & the National Endowment for the Humanities

  • Multilingual facet display

  • Development of static site export and researcher-facing exports

  • Development of work export features and analysis tools

  • Support for internationalization

Council of State Archivists

  • Support for the metadata creation feature

  • Development of the reviewer role and dashboard

  • Development of the spreadsheet interface

Civil War Governors of Kentucky Digital Documentary Edition & National Historic Records and Publications Commission

  • Funding for the advanced editor

  • Support for newly editable and exportable metadata fields

University of Texas-Austin Libraries

  • Security and accessibility testing

Beinecke Library at Yale University

  • Support for the bulk export feature

Church History Library (Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints) 

  • Funding multi-SSO integration

Civil War and Reconstruction Governors of Mississippi & National Historic Records and Publications Commission

  • Support developing the hierarchical subject exports

Folger Shakespeare Library

  • Support developing the head tag

  • Support for new TEI tags

Wellcome Library

  • Support for new TEI tags

Frederick Douglass Papers Project & the National Historic Records and Publications Commission

  • Support developing the footnote tag

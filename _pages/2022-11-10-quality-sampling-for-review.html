Enabling

Only the project owner can turn on quality sampling,

  1. Go to the Review tab of your collection.

  2. Click on "Quality Sampling". It will ask you to review a subset of pages.

  3. You can send the review URL to anyone who has permission to review in your project. If you a restricted review setting, you have to add people as reviewers. If you have an unrestricted review setting, then you can send it to anyone who can transcribe.

  4. You will see more data once you finish the quality sampling. That data scores FromThePage "works" so review time can be concentrated on material that may need more changes.

  5. Users marked as staff in FromThePage will also see quality scores for individual transcribers.

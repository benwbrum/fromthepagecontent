  1. If you have a private project, go to the Settings tab of your project page, scroll down to find API Access, and select "Open API".
  2. Click on one of the works in your project and go to the Download tab.
  3. Scroll down to the Experiment section. If you want to send your transcribed pages to Voyant, click the Analyze button beside it. If you want to send your transcribed pages to Word Trees, click the Analyze button beside it.

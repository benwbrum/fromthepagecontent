Suggestions For Creating Your Own Landing Page For Your Transcription Project

  • Explain the purpose and goals of the project to help potential volunteers quickly understand its significance.

  • Showcase the completed transcriptions from your project.

  • Share any milestones or progress your project has achieved.

  • If your project has partnered with organizations, universities, or experts, showcase those collaborations.

  • Use relevant images, diagrams, or graphics to enhance understanding.

  • Address common questions that volunteers might have about your transcription project.

  • Provide ways for volunteers to contact you or your team for inquiries or support.
